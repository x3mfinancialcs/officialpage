Legal & Data Privacy

Operating within Philippine financial regulations

We believe an informed borrower is a protected borrower. Here is a summary of how we operate and how your information is handled.

Regulatory Disclosure

X3M Financial Consultancy Services operates as a financial consultancy and loan facilitation service. We are not a bank and do not directly extend credit; loans are provided by accredited lending and financing partners in line with the Lending Company Regulation Act (RA 9474) and/or the Financing Company Act (RA 8556), as applicable.

TIN No.: 182-490-326-00000. DTI Business Name No.: 8094190 (view document).

Truth in Lending

In compliance with the Truth in Lending Act (RA 3765), all interest rates, finance charges, and loan terms are disclosed in writing before any agreement is signed. We encourage every client to review these disclosures carefully before proceeding.

Data Privacy

Personal and financial information submitted through this site is collected, used, and stored in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). It is used solely for loan processing and evaluation and is never sold to third parties. Full Privacy Policy.

Fair Collection Practices

We adhere to fair debt collection standards consistent with SEC and BSP guidelines, protecting borrowers from harassment, threats, or unfair collection tactics at every stage of a loan.
